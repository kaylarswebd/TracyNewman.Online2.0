Semaglutide Services

Semaglutide is a prescription medication originally developed to treat type 2 diabetes but has since been shown to help with significant weight loss as well. It works by mimicking a natural hormone in your body that regulates blood sugar levels, reduces appetite, and helps control food cravings. When used for weight management, Semaglutide has been clinically proven to help patients lose a substantial amount of weight by enhancing satiety and promoting healthier eating habits. Administered through a weekly injection, it is a convenient and effective tool for those struggling to achieve sustainable weight loss.

Semaglutide is a glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) agonist, which means it mimics the hormone GLP-1 that the body naturally produces. Semaglutide lowers blood sugar levels after meals by stimulating glucose-dependent insulin secretion. Semaglutide slows down the release of sugar into your blood, so your body in turn burns more fat.

Semaglutide also delays stomach emptying, which leaves you feeling full faster. You will also feel full for a longer period, which limits your overall calorie consumption. Semaglutide targets areas in the brain that are involved in appetite regulation. With this decrease in appetite, you will notice you don’t have your normal cravings. Most people notice less snacking throughout the day and less calorie consumption.

Several clinical studies show that weekly Semaglutide injections are highly effective at promoting weight loss. In one study, individuals treated with Semaglutide lost an average of 9-13% of their body weight over a year without making any changes to their diet and exercise habits. However, it is shown that diet and exercise habits play a key part in receiving this medication.

Before/During Your Treatment

Get lab work done within 6 months of treatment (comprehensive metabolic panel, complete blood count (CBC), thyroid panel, lipid panel, and hemoglobin A1C) with your primary care provider.

During your appointment, Tracy will explain the procedure and answer any questions. We will discuss whether you are a candidate for Semaglutide, what your health goals are, and make an individualized and thorough treatment plan. We will take body composition measurements before administering the medication. The actual injection of Semaglutide will take seconds and is relatively painless.

After Treatment

You may experience mild side effects after your first few doses including gastrointestinal upset, nausea, heartburn, stomach pain, diarrhea, constipation, or dizziness. Your provider will be in contact with you weekly as needed to ensure your dosing is correct and to help manage any side effects you may experience. Your dosing will increase monthly if you are tolerating the dosing, which is further explained in your consult and education. You will be periodically visiting our office to monitor your body composition as you continue on Semaglutide.

Is This Service Right For Me?

Semaglutide is for individuals who are looking for a medical way to manage their weight. You must be in good health, have recent lab work done, and have a BMI of 27 or above. If you have a BMI less than 27, you may still be considered for Semaglutide – this will be determined in your initial consultation. For Semaglutide to be fully beneficial, a healthy diet is necessary, as well as regular exercise. We recommend at least 30 minutes of exercise daily and/or taking 10,000 steps a day. If you are not able to commit to a healthier lifestyle, you may want to reconsider if this is a safe or viable option for you.
